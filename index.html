Pokemon Champions Dex, Team Builder, Moves, Items and Events

DexPro - Champions is a free unofficial Pokemon Champions database and competitive toolkit. Use it to browse the Pokemon Champions Pokemon Dex, learnable moves, ability descriptions, held items, Mega Stones, type matchups, weakness charts, seasons, mystery gifts, and online competitions.

The app is built for players who want quick Pokemon Champions references while planning teams, checking type coverage, reviewing event rewards, or comparing move and ability data.
